Intelligent asphalt pavement simulation system

An educational system for simulations based on different conditions with various methods. This is a commercial system that I designed for a Chinese company, and this system is developed and will be put into a real application.

This project allows me to follow the requirements and the facts. I need to communicate with customers and balance my ideas with funding.

During work, I have to meet the requirements and tastes of the customers. So, I focus on the interface design and if I want to add some functions, I have to communicate with my employer and customers. I add limited ideas because I need to balance funding and thoughts.

During this process, I iterated on three stages and suggested my own ideas, and finally, the customers accepted some of these ideas and be satisfied with the whole system.
