Intelligent asphalt pavement simulation system

A simulation instructional system that uses a variety of techniques and settings to simulate material, environment, structure, and more. This is a commercial system that I developed for a Chinese company and will be used in a real-world application.

Customers' needs and preferences were met throughout the project. I concentrated on interface design while communicating with my employer and customers. My ideas were added after the project had been iterated three times.

Early Prototyping Stage

The placements, sizes, and functions are arranged accordingly.

In the following stage, it is enhanced to be high-fidelity, visible, add backdrops, and rearrange colors.

Final design

Some details, such as the display of outcome data and the checkbox, are being improved in order to be consistent and clean.
