The Product

An app designed to provide a launchpad for emerging artists, allowing them to upload their music and receive votes and feedback. We facilitate discovery of new talents and provide a social platform for users to interact and engage with each other.

Initial Prototype

By producing the first prototype, I demonstrate all of our ideas by creating this prototype.

User Testing

Interview users based on our first prototype.

Main Goal
  • To test the effectiveness and benefits of having the “voting” feature for both independent artists and fans.
  • To see whether our app helps users find new music and new independent artists.
Takeaways

  • Recommendation: Explain the "voting" feature and make it clear and easy to understand in the UI.

    5/5 users had trouble understanding the purpose of tokens and voting without a verbal explanation, it is clear that our prototype's UI failed to explain our "voting" feature to our users.

  • The pinwheel feature was effective at helping users find new music and new independent artists. But only if they liked the music, they want to discover the profile and story of that artist.

Discovery

With the Discovery tab, users can discover new artists by scrolling through the interactive pinwheel.
They can click on the profile picture of the artists they like and learn everything they want to know about them.

  • Discover artists or songs, easily switch between them
  • Scroll to discover
  • Tap to play music
  • Drag to view profile, story, etc.

*Before: Tap - View profile
Now: Tap - play music Drag - View Profile
After user testing, we realize that only if users liked the music did they want to discover the profile of the artist.

Events

They get notified about where and when their favorite artists are performing and they can buy tickets directly from the app.

  • View events
  • Buy tickets

Community

With the Community tab, users can engage with other fans or artists.
They can find like-minded individuals and share their love for music and feel more connected to them and more eager to contribute to their growth when they use the community feature.

  • View & Join Community to enjoy (Add incentives like unlock newest music/discount to attract users to join)
  • See current discussions with their favorite artists and see who is online
  • Chat with the artist or fans

Vote

Users can vote for their favorite artists. They can earn free tokens or buy tokens to vote, and check current ranking. The more votes an artist gets, the more exposure they get. And we will hold concerts for top-ranked artists.

  • Vote to support artists
  • Buy or earn tokens to vote
  • See current ranking
  • Concerts for top-ranked artists

My Music

With the My Music tab, they can easily download and listen to their favorite tracks.
Users can create their own personal library and experience music the way they want to.
