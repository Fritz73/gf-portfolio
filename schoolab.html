SCHOOLAB

A product inclusivity self-evaluation system that raises awareness about product inclusion and encourages more people to use Schoolab services.

Company SCHOOLAB
Timeline Aug - Dec 2022
My Role Individual work in UI/UX design;
Research and principle development

Problem Statement

In order to encourage more businesses to employ Schoolab and make the market's products inclusive, we need to:

  • Develop criteria of design principles to build products more inclusively.
  • Show the company how they can improve the inclusivity of their product.
  • Convince them that their product can benefit by consulting with Schoolab.
  • Help Schoolab understand the product's inclusive strengths and weaknesses so that they can help them.

Stakeholder Map

I created a stakeholder map to identify the vulnerable users to research.

Research Method

We interviewed 12 users, made a survey, and analyzed the competitors.

Key Insights

We found that a company face at list one of these problems:

  • Internal Discord
    The absence of DEI is dividing employees throughout the organization. The corporation seeks out Schoolab to enhance employee satisfaction, fix broken systems, and promote employee cohesion in order to lessen internal conflict.
  • Outside Optics
    An organization has to enhance how partners, investors, and/or customers view them. This company's staff and goods are not equipped with a competent DEI plan.
  • Financial Achievement
    The business is having problems connecting with customers, selling products to a certain demographic, or developing products that are not the ideal fit for that group of customers.

Principle Ideate

To address these issues, we brainstormed design principles and criteria to build more inclusive products.
arrow_forward arrow_downward
After identifying the fundamental ideas, we purposefully created criteria and divided them into four quadrants, functional, physical, requirement, and psychological, as inclusive product features.
arrow_forward arrow_downward
To evaluate these principles, we later added criteria such as who it affects, the impact on products, and the impact on stakeholders.

Product Analysis

To examine specific features that influenced inclusivity, we studied at 50 products.

Principle Extract

We selected and summarized 6 most critical factors, including the definition, examples, who it affects, and how stakeholders are affected.

Final Prototype

Allow product creators to self-assess the inclusiveness of their products in order to raise awareness of the importance of inclusivity and entice them to try Schoolab.

Using process:

  1. To gauge inclusion, the clients conduct a brief survey.
  2. View the results of their inclusive products in each category
  3. Learn general recommendations
  4. By selecting Schoolab, customers can increase their inclusive performance.

Evaluate

Completing a short, ten-question survey and clicking "Submit" to get the results.

Gain Result

Viewing the included products' scores in each aspect and discover each principle's definition.

Learn Recommendation

Learning broad suggestions for implementation, impacts, and how stakeholders are affected.

Schoolab Service

Contact Schoolab or decide to try the Schoolab Service.

Choose from three different services for customers.

View Schoolab's prior accomplishments.

Choose to pay immediately, view the previous business cases, or get in touch.

Can also return to view their own result.

Next Step

Apply this design to the Schoolab website.

Promote it so that more businesses can utilize and experiment with it.

Impact

Make more product creators aware of the importance of inclusivity.
Assist them in understanding their specific issues.
Encourage more businesses to use Schoolab.
Make products inclusive.
In the future, create an inclusive market.
