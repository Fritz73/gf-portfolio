Pet Together

An all-encompassing internet platform for pet lovers that integrates community, entertainment, life*, and pet welfare, allowing for entirely new socializing opportunities.

Concept

A one-stop solution offering everything centered around pets, whether you want to play games, donate, find lost pets, choose services, make friends, or enjoy the information about pets, this APP has got you covered.
Here, all things about pets are available!

Problem Statement

To have an interesting and convenient life with their pets, pet owners and pet lovers need:

  • A way to connect
  • Supervise
  • Find services and goods

Considerations

How to attract users? How to maintain users?
How to invite stores/services/online doctors?
Who pays for us? Competition? Market? Technology?
Pets cannot use the app themselves, only owners!

Problem

Due to the limited living space in cities, owners or lovers may not be able to provide their pets with the most beautiful home, but they still have this dream.

Solution

This app allows pet owners to decorate their online homes, arrange and share their posts.

Contact and make new friends

Problem

Young people in the city want to exchange experiences or make friends with other people who like pets because it is not so easy to socialize offline without knowing how to meet them.

Solution

Find nearby buddies for your pet, join groups, and make friends through your pet.
Additionally, contacting store owners, physicians, admirers, or random strangers is acceptable.

Online/Offline goods and services

Problem

Pet owners do not know which pet store to choose when they need them because the market is cluttered with different products and lacks focus.
While the veterinary clinic is closed at midnight, pet owners is anxious about their sick pets.

Solution

Get offline and online services and products for the duration of your pet's life, including during death, celebrations, online doctor visits (AI physicians for common illnesses), and other pet-related events. Purchase pet insurance right now to protect against pet illness.
